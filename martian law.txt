THE CONSTITUTION AND CODE OF CONDUCT OF THE MARTIAN COLONY

===============================================================================
Title 0: SUPREME CONSTITUTIONAL FOUNDATION AND RATIFICATION
===============================================================================

Article 001: The Supreme Status and Amendment Thresholds
* This foundational text is declared absolute, true, and just. It stands as the supreme framework for all life within the Martian zone of influence.

The Removal Threshold:
* Altering, repealing, or removing any existing portion of this Constitution requires an 80% supermajority vote of all eligible voters across the entire colony. A failure to cast a ballot counts as a automatic vote for rejection. If 21% or more of the electorate does not show up to vote, the proposed removal is defeated for a period of 100 Martial years.

The Addition Threshold:
* Introducing additional rights or new articles requires a 75% supermajority vote under the exact same calculation rules. Every single eligible voter must be counted in the baseline total.

Article 002: Calendar Synchronization
* All legal timelines, dates, notice periods, and operational durations dictated throughout this entire Constitution are based strictly on the Martian calendar unless a specific statute explicitly defines the measurement in Earth years.

Article 003: The Citizen's Binding Oath and Duty
* Upon reaching the legal voting age of 30 (or age of 20 for military candidacy alignment), every single citizen must read, fully comprehend, and swear a binding legal oath to the state.

The Good-Faith Mandate: Citizens swear to uphold the Constitution and its code of conduct in good faith to the absolute best of their physical and mental abilities.

The Reporting Requirement: This oath establishes a direct legal responsibility to actively report any observed crimes, acts of corruption, or environmental hazards immediately to the appropriate public safety authorities. Silently witnessing a crime or turning a blind eye violates the oath and threatens the survival of the collective.

Article 004: The Presumption of Legality and Universal Irrelevance
To ensure absolute clarity and prevent the over-regulation of private life, the state establishes the baseline rule of personal freedom:

* The Presumption of Legality: Any action, choice, or conduct that is not explicitly banned or restricted by this Constitution or its active statutes is completely legal. The state shall never prosecute a citizen for an act that has not been clearly codified as a crime.

* The Categorical Safety Exceptions: The presumption of legality does not apply to categories explicitly locked down by the state for colony survival. Any item or process falling under a blanket ban—specifically unauthorized mind-altering substances (Article 2001), projectile or explosive weaponry (Article 301), and unauthorized biological or hazardous materials (Article 1902)—is automatically prohibited unless explicitly permitted on the state ledger.

* The Irrelevance of Demographics: Because survival requires the maximum utility of every capable individual, traits such as race (whether human or non-human sapient species), ethnicity, biological sex are non-existent concepts to the state. They are completely irrelevant to a citizen’s legal rights, duties, or standing.

Article 005: The State Monopoly on Violence and Enforcement
The Martian state retains an absolute, non-negotiable monopoly on the administration of violence, corporal punishment, and criminal correction.

* The Subversion Penalty: Any attempt by a private citizen, corporate entity, or un-deputized collective to subvert this monopoly—including vigilante justice, running unauthorized penal operations, or executing unsanctioned physical punishments—is a severe crime against the state.

* Impersonation of Officials: Intentionally impersonating any state official within the executive branch—including, but not limited to, law enforcement officers, judges, or military personnel—shall be treated as a direct assault on state sovereignty.

* The Two-Brand Sanction: Conviction for subverting the state monopoly or impersonating an executive official carries a mandatory sentence of two criminal markings administered to the forearm under Article 204, alongside the corresponding mandatory lashings. This places the offender exactly one strike away from the airlock.

* Brawls and Mutual Combat: Ordinary physical altercations, street brawls, or personal fights that do not attempt to usurp state authority or enforce vigilante "punishments" do not violate this monopoly. They are classified as minor public nuisances and are penalized strictly by lashings only, with no permanent markings applied.

Article 006: The Directing Principle of Crimes and Retribution
To ensure the judiciary applies punishments with absolute consistency, the state codifies the foundational organizing principle behind all criminal classification:

Attacks on the Individual: Hostile acts, disputes, or offenses directed strictly against an individual citizen—such as minor assaults, personal brawls, or localized theft—are classified as lesser infractions. Unless these acts become systemic, habitual, or predatory, they are restricted to Tier 1 and Tier 2 physical or institutional corrections. The state will not expand capital resources on private, non-existential grievances.

Attacks on the State and Collective Survival: Any offense that attacks the structure, security, credibility, fidelity, or long-term viability of the state—or harms its people by proxy—is classified as a high crime against collective survival. This principle dictates why crimes like public corruption or drug trafficking bypass lesser sanctions entirely: they erode the social contract, sabotage state legitimacy, and introduce systemic vulnerabilities that could adversely affect the population for generations.

The Three-Tier Penal Structure
The organizing principle dictates three rigid, non-negotiable tiers of criminal retribution based strictly on the target and systemic impact of the offense:

Tier 1: Minor Offenses (Public Nuisances)
Target: Individual peace, labor disputes, or localized compliance. No threat to groups of people, life-spupport or state owned entities.
Retribution: Upon conviction, immediate public lashings on the back via automated machinery under Article 204. Once delivered, the individual is free.

Tier 2: Intermediate Offenses (Gross Negligence & Structural Harms)
Target: Safety infrastructure, physical habitats, or repeat individual crimes or otherwise stated. Any kind of fraud.
Retribution: Mandatory public lashings combined with a permanent, distinct physical brand administered to the inside of the forearm under Article 204. Receiving a third brand triggers automatic escalation to capital punishment.

Tier 3: High Crimes (Existential Sabotage & Treason)
Target: The sovereignty of the state, public health, resource safety, or the general public
Retribution: Mandatory capital punishment executed via exile through the primary airlock or forced displacement outside the perimeter with no return transport. If the planet is terraformed to the point of where life is possible outside, execution via public hanging.

Post-Deputization Accountability and Fraudulent Intent Clause

* The Mandatory Post-Action Investigation: If this Constitution ever activates a supreme mandate that deputizes any or all citizens to neutralize an existential threat against itself (such as under the Article 2708 shadow-agency purge), the state shall launch a deep, exhaustive, and thorough forensic investigation into every single action taken by the public once stability is restored.

* The Good-Faith False Positive Standard: If a citizen mistakes a set of circumstances for an active constitutional violation, identifies a false positive, and carries out a terminal execution, they shall face a strict tribunal. If the investigation proves the citizen honestly, reasonably, and demonstrably believed they were acting in good faith for the absolute protection of the public interest, they shall be spared Tier 3 execution. Instead, they shall receive a minimum of a Tier 1 automated lashing or, at worst, a Tier 2 forearm branding depending on the severity of their error.

* The Fraudulent Pretext Penalty: If any citizen maliciously attempts to exploit a public deputization event as a protective shield to commit unauthorized murder, settle personal vendettas, or hide criminal intent to avoid justice, they shall be stripped of all legal defense. They shall be permanently branded as a traitor to the state and executed under the mandatory capital provisions of Tier 3.

TITLE I: GOVERNANCE AND CIVIC BURDEN
===============================================================================

Article 101: The Martian Council
Democratic elections shall occur exactly every five Martian years to form the governing council.

Article 102: Candidate Eligibility
Citizens must meet all the following criteria to run for public office:
* Must be at least 30 Earth years of age.
* Must not exceed 65 Earth years of age.
* Must hold a completely clear criminal record.
* Must be a currently registered and active voter.
* Must not have served 2 or more terms previously

Article 103: The Doctrine of Civic Burden
Governance is legally classified as a heavy civic burden, not a privilege. Elected officials shall receive no luxury bonuses, specialized rations, or elevated social status. Only people who can vote can serve. Elected officals are public servants first, leaders second.

* Trust and Fidelity Alignment: In accordance with the guiding principles of Article 006, any elected official who uses their position for personal enrichment or corporate favor commits a Tier 3 High Crime. Because they are servants entrusted with the survival of the collective, any breach of that trust erodes the foundational credibility of the state and shall be met with absolute retribution

Article 104: Electoral Integrity, Secret Ballots, and Voting Protections
To safeguard the foundational democratic process and maintain the absolute fidelity of the state, all public elections shall be conducted using uncompromising, high-integrity physical standards:

* The Universal Voting Expectation: All currently eligible voters are expected to participate in regular Martian Council elections. No entity, manager, or employer shall under any circumstance bar, delay, or penalize a worker for exercising their right to vote. In exchange, the state shall strictly organize election cycles to fall upon standard rest days to prevent labor disruptions.

* The Paper Ballot Mandate: Voting shall be performed exclusively via physical ink-on-paper ballots (or equivalents). The use of digital systems, automated voting machines, or network-connected terminals is entirely prohibited to eliminate the risk of systemic electronic rigging or software manipulation

* On-the-Spot Verification: Before receiving a paper ballot, every prospective voter shall be subjected to strict, on-the-spot physical and cryptographic identity verification by election clerks to confirm active registration and prevent voter fraud.

* The Anonymity Protocol: To guarantee absolute secrecy, the voter shall place their marked paper ballot inside a completely non-distinct inner slip or envelope. This inner container shall then be deposited directly into a locked, untampered master ballot chest

* The Voting Booth Sanctuary: The immediate polling station and voting booth are declared absolute sanctuaries of free choice.No entity shall coerce, intimidate, or attempt to influence a voter at their place of voting. Attempting to manipulate a voter at the booth constitutes a direct act of political sabotage and an erosion of public trust, classified as a mandatory Tier 3 High Crime or considered act of war where relevant.

* The Adversarial Counting Committee: Once voting concludes, ballots shall be counted by an electoral committee comprised directly of opposing political candidates or their verified, designated representatives. This ensures a transparent, adversarial system where each faction actively audits the other.

* The Absolute Accountability Lock: At any point, time, and location, the absolute integrity of the vote must be actively safeguarded by all personnel working at the voting center. If the forensic count reveals even a single discrepancy between the physical number of paper ballots cast and the number of voter registration sign-offs on the secure ledger, the entire ballot lot from that location is legally compromised. The lot shall be thrown out and securely destroyed immediately, and a mandatory re-vote shall be scheduled at the next open opportunity.

* Slip Invalidation and Defacement: The specific voting standard agreed upon by the state shall be rigidly enforced. Any ballot that is defaced, contains multiple marks where not permitted, or features marking indicators placed in a non-permissible configuration is legally invalidated, and shall be placed in a sparate pile for the final forensic counting.

* Electoral Interference and Terminal Sanctions: Any deliberate attempt to manipulate election outcomes at the voting center—including, but not limited to, ballot-box stuffing, the unauthorized insertion of slips, intentional miscounting, or structural tampering with the ledger—constitutes an existential threat to the colony. It is classified as a Tier 3 High Crime, carrying the mandatory capital execution penalty.

TITLE II: CRIMES AND CAPITAL PUNISHMENT
===============================================================================

Article 201: Capital Offenses
The Martian colony maintains absolute zero tolerance for destabilizing crimes. The following offenses carry a mandatory, non-negotiable death sentence:
* Premeditated murder
* Sexual assault or rape
* Crimes against minors (Pedophilia)
* Public corruption or bribery
* Terrorism or sabotage of colony infrastructure
* High treason against the settlement
* Unauthorized trafficking of controlled substances
* Slavers
* Material support for those that are convicted under this Article

Article 202: Method of Execution (The Airlock Provision)
Until Mars possesses a naturally breathable atmosphere, the colony will not waste life-support resources on death row inmates. Execution shall be carried out exclusively via permanent exile:
* Option A: Immediate expulsion through the primary airlock without a pressure suit.
* Option B: Forced transit to a distance of 100 kilometers outside the colony perimeter with no return transport.

Article 203: Economic and Institutional Shunning Protocol
When a person or legal entity is sentenced to institutional shunning, they are not completely barred from conducting business or interacting with the state. Instead, they are subjected to a strict 10-Martian-year economic restriction:
 * The Competitor Mandate: State agencies and public offices must actively avoid doing business with the shunned entity. If any viable, competing alternative exists on the market, the alternative must be chosen automatically.The 20% Evaluation Threshold: The state may only break this protocol and deal with a shunned entity if the entity's goods or services are more than 20% cheaper or 20% better in absolute, objectively measurable terms compared to all competitors.
 * Duration: This punitive economic restriction remains actively enforced for a non-negotiable period of exactly 10 Martian years

Article 204: Execution of Corporal Punishment and Criminal Markings
The physical delivery of state-sanctioned discipline must follow precise, standardized parameters to maintain absolute judicial objectivity:

*Lashing Protocols: Minor offenses are penalized via public lashings delivered strictly to the offender's back. The punishment must never maim, break bones, or permanently disfigure the individual

*To completely eliminate human bias, zealotry, or variance in physical strength, lashings shall be executed via automated machines, resource and technology levels permitting. The precise volume and kinetic force of the lashings are strictly determined by the sentencing courts under Title XIII.

*Marking Design and Intent: Permanent markings must use a highly distinct, universally recognized shape that communicates the crime at a glance. Because the state expects and encourages offenders to reform their behavior, these markings are placed on the inside of the left forearm where they can be modestly hidden by everyday clothing during regular social interaction.

*Inspection Rights: State marshals, law enforcement officers, and authorized public officials maintain the absolute legal right to demand a physical inspection of a citizen's marking locations at any time.

*Anatomical Exceptions: If the left forearm is entirely unavailable due to combat injury, amputation, or congenital birth defects, the branding machine shall administer the mark on the inside of the right forearm, or the next closest viable surface.

TITLE III: SECURITY AND ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION
===============================================================================

Article 301: Projectile Weapon Ban
The unauthorized possession, manufacture, or import of projectile firearms, kinetic ballistics, or explosive weaponry by any civilian, corporation, or private entity is strictly illegal
*The Military Exception: This restriction applies strictly to non-state entities. The state military and authorized state defense forces are explicitly permitted to manufacture, store, and utilize projectile and explosive weaponry. These weapon systems are recognized as necessary tools for planetary defense and the enforcement of state sovereignty.
* Justification: Any hull puncture causes immediate decompression and catastrophic loss of life.

*Storage and Safe Zoning: In accordance with the hazardous research standards of Title XIX, all state-owned manufacturing facilities, weapon depots, and live-fire military testing ranges for projectile systems must be isolated completely outside core civilian habitation modules. They must be located at the designated safe perimeter as designated under Title XIX

* Duration: The absolute ban on civilian possession and private manufacturing remains active until the Martian atmosphere is certified by state experts as fully breathable.

TITLE IV: FAMILY, AGE, AND SOCIAL STANDARDS
===============================================================================

Article 401: Domestic Structure
The colony legally recognizes only monogamous unions. Polygamy is strictly prohibited to ensure demographic and legal stability.

Article 402: Age of Maturity
The age of legal consent for all personal, medical, and sexual decisions is set strictly at 18 Earth years of age.

Article 403: Controlled Substances
The purchase, possession, and consumption of alcohol is restricted to individuals who are 18 Earth years of age or older.

Article 404: Conscription and Voting Rights
* Military Age: Mandatory or voluntary military enlistment begins at age 20 (Earth years).
* Voting Provision: All active military personnel are automatically granted the right to vote. Old enough to go to war, old enough to vote.
* Disqualification: Active servicemen are explicitly banned from running for elected office to maintain the separation of military and civilian rule.

Article 405: The Productivity Mandate
In a closed ecosystem with finite resources, survival requires maximum efficiency. Individuals who actively refuse to work, malinger, or act as useless dregs on community resources shall face institutional shunning. This includes the loss of recreational privileges, reduction to base-level survival rations, and social isolation.

===============================================================================
TITLE V: CIVIL LIBERTIES AND PUBLIC PROTECTIONS
===============================================================================

Article 501: Freedom of Expression
Every citizen maintains the absolute right to freedom of speech, opinion, and expression. The governing council shall pass no law restricting the peaceful sharing of ideas, criticisms, or beliefs.

Article 502: The Right to Peacefully Assemble
Citizens have the right to protest and assemble.
* Notice Requirement: Organizers must submit a notice three Martian weeks prior to the event.
* Automatic Approval: Once the notice period passes, approval is automatically granted. The state cannot deny or block the assembly.

Article 503: Plain Language Requirement
To ensure universal access to justice, all Martian laws, regulations, and legal notices must be written strictly in plain, accessible English. The use of dense legal jargon or intentionally confusing terminology is unconstitutional so that everybody can understand them.

===============================================================================
TITLE VI: CORPORATE ACCOUNTABILITY AND ANTI-CORRUPTION
===============================================================================

Article 601: Non-Personhood of Corporations
Under no circumstances shall a corporation, collective enterprise, or any legal entity (excluding sole proprietorships) be granted the rights, liberties, or status of a natural person.
* Purpose: Corporations do not possess human rights. They exist solely to serve the colony and bear responsibilities, not privileges.

Article 602: Total Ban on Corporate Lobbying
Any attempt by a corporation or its agents to influence legislation, policy, or politicians through financial means, gifts, or favors is legally defined as corruption.
* The Dual Penalty: Both the corporate executive offering the influence and the politician accepting it shall receive the mandatory capital punishment outlined in Article 201 (Exile via airlock).

Article 603: Absolute Liability for Politicians
Elected officials and politicians receive zero legal protection, immunity, or special privileges against crimes. They are subject to the exact same laws as ordinary citizens and shall face immediate prosecution for any criminal acts.

Article 604: Democratized Criminal Pardons
No individual—neither the Council leader, a judge, nor a commander—holds the power to pardon a convicted criminal. Pardons can only be granted through a public, colony-wide election.

===============================================================================
TITLE VII: LAW ENFORCEMENT AND DEFENSE OF HABITAT
===============================================================================

Article 701: Police Training Standards
To ensure a highly disciplined and lawful force, all law enforcement officers must complete a mandatory 3-to-4 Earth-year training program, resource levels permitting.

Article 702: Qualified Shielding and Misconduct
* Operational Protection: Police officers receive general legal shielding from civil liability while performing their duties in good faith.
* Forfeiture of Protection: If an officer commits a crime, abuses their authority, or violates civil rights, their legal shielding is immediately and permanently stripped. They shall be prosecuted as ordinary citizens.

Article 703: The Habitat Castle Doctrine
A citizen's habitat module is their absolute sanctuary. If an intruder breaks into a private habitat, they immediately forfeit their right to life.
* Right to Terminate: The habitat owner is legally permitted to use lethal force to neutralize the intruder instantly.
* Prohibited Actions: The owner may only terminate the threat. Taking the intruder alive to inflict torture, unlawful imprisonment, or sexual assault remains strictly illegal and punishable under Article 201.

===============================================================================
TITLE VIII: DOMESTIC RELATIONS AND CHILD WELFARE
===============================================================================

Article 801: Dissolution of Marriage
Marriage is legally defined as a binding civil contract.
* Prohibition of No-Fault Divorce: The state does not recognize "no-fault" divorce. To dissolve a marriage, one party must legally prove fault.
* Recognized Faults: Valid grounds for divorce are strictly limited to physical assault, infidelity, or the criminal conviction of a spouse.
* Alimony Ban: The concept of alimony is completely abolished. Neither party shall ever be legally required to financially support a former spouse.

Article 802: Child Custody and Support
* Baseline Status: The state mandates automatic shared custody of all children following a divorce.
* Exceptions to Custody: Shared custody is only revoked if a parent is demonstrably unfit due to clinical insanity, active imprisonment, or death.
* Child Support Ban: Because shared custody is the mandatory baseline, child support payments do not exist.
* Anti-Weaponization Mandate: Child custody and support shall never be weaponized for vindictive or personal gain. Any parent found attempting to exploit the child for legal or financial leverage against the other parent shall immediately forfeit all custody rights.

TITLE IX: EDUCATION, IDEOLOGY, AND RELIGION
===============================================================================

Article 901: Neutrality in Education and Public Service
All childcare facilities, schools, and public administrative offices must remain completely free of religious, political, or ideological influence.
* Enforcement: Any teacher, childcare professional, or state clerk who introduces religious or ideological bias into their professional duties shall be immediately stripped of their titles, state grants, and employment.

State Recognition: The Martian state recognizes only its own constitution. No external political, philosophical, or moral frameworks hold authority.Article 902: Privacy of Religious BeliefEvery citizen possesses the freedom to practice their religion, but it must remain a strictly private affair.Public Restrictions: Religious practices, symbols, and expressions are strictly forbidden in public spaces, with the sole exception of small, designated zones specifically zoned for religious gatherings.Prohibition of Religious Infrastructure: The construction of public churches, mosques, temples, or other prominent religious buildings is entirely prohibited. Religious belief is respected like any other deeply personal matter: it belongs to the individual, and the public has no desire or obligation to engage with it.

TITLE X: STATE SOVEREIGNTY AND ANTI-CORRUPTION
===============================================================================

Article 1001: Absolute Sovereignty
*The Martian colony recognizes itself as the sole ruling authority. It rejects all legal claims, treaties, or sovereign mandates issued by any government, corporation, or entities.

Article 1002: Systemic Corruption and Severe Retribution

*Systemic corruption within any legal entity or branch of government—including taking bribes, trading favors, or intentionally manipulating the letter of the law to bypass its intent—shall be met with harsh and absolute retribution.
*Scope of Investigation: Investigation teams shall leave no stone unturned to root out corruption. The scope is all included parties, their families and known friends and associates to the 3rd degree of separation.

*Complicity and Family Assets:
* If an official is found guilty, any family members or associates who knowingly benefited from or actively aided the corrupt enterprise shall be thoroughly investigated, stripped of their assets, and rooted out of public society alongside the primary offender.

===============================================================================
ITLE XI: STATUTORY INTERPRETATION AND JUDICIAL AMBIGUITY
===============================================================================

Article 1101: Statutory Intent
All laws are written to be harsh but fair, prioritizing the continued survival and long-term well-being of the colony. While laws are drafted with the maximum possible clarity to eliminate ambiguity, the state acknowledges that no written text is completely flawless.

Article 1102: The Constitutional Interpretation Jury
Where a law is found to be genuinely ambiguous, its interpretation shall not be decided by judges or politicians. Instead, interpretation is handled on a case-by-case basis by a specialized citizen jury.

*Jury Composition: A panel of 100 people shall be selected using the highest standard of mathematical randomization, excluding parties close to the case.

*Demographics: The pool includes every citizen from the age of 16 until death, including individuals with prior criminal records. Only those proven to be clinically mentally unwell are excluded.

*Verdict Threshold: To establish a legal interpretation for a case, a supermajority of 70% or more of the jury must agree.

*Abolition of Case Law: The concept of "case law" or legal precedent is abolished. The jury's decision applies strictly to that specific case and does not dictate how future laws are interpreted.

TITLE XII: ASSET FORFEITURE AND CORPORATE SUCCESSION
===============================================================================

Article 1201: Continuity of Operations
If a corporate executive, owner, or agent is executed under Article 602 for corruption or lobbying, or any other reason, the day-to-day operations of the company shall proceed as normal to prevent societal disruption.

Article 1202: Succession and Nationalization
Corporate assets shall only face state intervention under specific circumstances:

*The Heir Provision: If the executed party was the sole owner or majority shareholder, the company passes directly to their legal heirs, provided the heirs are completely innocent of the crime.

*State Dissolution: If there is no legal heir, the state reserves the right to nationalize the assets or dissolve the company entirely if it is found to provide no structural value to the colony.

*The Worker Protection Mandate: During any asset forfeiture, dissolution, or restructuring, the state must explicitly prioritize the physical and economic well-being of innocent workers employed by the entity.

TITLE XIII: CORRECTIONAL JUSTICE AND PHYSICAL SANCTIONS
===============================================================================

Article 1301: Prohibition of Long-Term Incarceration
Because life-support, space, and food resources are strictly finite, the state shall not maintain long-term prisons or jails. Criminal sentences must be resolved quickly, efficiently, and with minimal strain on the colony's life-support systems.

Article 1302: Minor Offenses (Corporal Punishment)
Minor offenses that do not threaten the core safety of the habitat shall be penalized via public corporal punishment. The standard penalty is a designated number of lashes with a whip, administered immediately following conviction. Once the punishment is delivered, the citizen is returned to their normal life.

Article 1303: Intermediate Offenses (The Branding Sanction)
Serious offenses that fall just below the threshold of capital punishment shall be met with permanent physical branding alongside mandatory lashings, in severe cases institutional shunning.

*Method: Branding shall be executed using a hot or cold iron, placed exclusively on the inside of the offender's left forearm.

*The Three-Strike Rule: This permanent marker serves as a direct public warning. Receiving a third brand triggers automatic escalation to capital punishment (Exile via airlock under Article 202).

*The Maturity Exemption: To protect developing minds, branding is strictly prohibited on any individual below the age of 25 Earth years. Minor offenders under 25 (earth years) shall instead receive standard corporal punishment or reduced institutional shunning to 2 years.

After this extension, the asset enters the public domain permanently.Article 1403: The Trademark Evaluation CommitteeTo determine if a modification is a "minor tweak," a temporary evaluation committee of exactly three field experts shall be formed.Strict Neutrality: Committee members must have zero direct or indirect connection to the trademark registrant. If systemic monetary corruption is uncovered, members of the general public are legally deputized to bypass standard bureaucracy and execute the corrupt apparatus root and stem. This is classified as both a supreme civic right and an absolute survival responsibility.

TITLE XVI: SOVEREIGN DEBT AND ANTI-CAPTURE MEASURES
===============================================================================

Article 1601: The Debt-Free Imperative
The Martian state must actively strive to remain completely debt-free. A colony burdened by financial debt is structurally vulnerable. The state is strictly prohibited from becoming beholden, financially or politically, to foreign entities or interests.
Article 1602: Banknote Issuance and Wealth Capture Limits
The Currency Distinction: The issuance of state-backed banknotes to private citizens shall be managed solely to facilitate local internal trade, or safe, state backed investment.

The Financial Hostage Provision: The state shall maintain strict regulatory control mechanisms over high-value capital pools. No private citizen or consolidated group of wealthy individuals may accumulate enough economic leverage to hold the state financially hostage.

Treasonous Accumulation: Any deliberate attempt by a private citizen to use concentrated wealth to coerce, manipulate, or destabilize state operations or monetary systems constitutes high treason. Conviction carries the mandatory execution penalty under Article 201.

TITLE XVIII: MATERIAL STANDARDS AND ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION
===============================================================================

Article 1801: The Iron Alloy Mandate
Because petrochemical plastics (such as PET) and refined glass represent structural resource bottlenecks on Mars, manufacturing must prioritize raw durability.Material Priority: Industrial and consumer packaging, storage vessels, bottles, and cups must be manufactured from highly recyclable iron alloys wherever structurally feasible.Sustainability Standard: All manufactured items must optimize for infinite reusability and mechanical recycling to conserve closed-loop colony elements.
Article 1802: Life-Critical Environmental Negligence
Every citizen and legal entity bears an absolute responsibility to protect the closed ecosystem. *Discretionary Tariff Power: The state may levy customs duties, regulatory fees, or structural import restrictions on any incoming external goods at any rate—or choose to apply no rate—at its absolute statutory discretion

*The Un-Docking Restriction: Cargo assessments and taxation protocols shall occur at the port of entry upon arrival. To ensure practical logic, a vessel must first be allowed to dock to undergo assessment and processing. However, once docked and taxed, no vessel shall be permitted to un-dock, discharge its cargo into the main habitat grids, or break planetary orbit until its specific tariff balance is certified as fully settled.

Title XXX: Extra-Habitat Commercial Operations and Space Law
=======================================================

Article 3001: Extension of Sovereign Labor and Environmental Jurisdictions
To prevent corporate exploitation and preserve structural safety beyond core civilian modules, all mining, salvage, deep-space harvesting, and heavy commercial activities operating within the Martian zone of influence (ZOI) are bound strictly to the supreme laws of this state:

*The Universal Labor Extension: All labor protections, shift rest schedules, shift limits, parental leave guarantees, commuting subsidies, and wage-theft protections outlined under Title XXIII apply to every crew member, asteroid miner, and technician, without regard to their distance from the planet's surface.

*The Safety and Protective Equipment Mandate: Commercial entities operating orbital refineries, surface strip-mines, or deep-space salvaging rigs must provide maximum-grade personal protective equipment (PPE) and maintain robust corporate workplace insurance. Any attempt to cut corners on structural life-support safety, vacuum-suits, or radiation shields shall be prosecuted under the Engineering Integrity Mandate (Article 1804).

*Hazardous Waste and Environmental Liability: The strict protocols of Title XVIII and Title XIX regarding life-critical environmental negligence, the prohibition of "forever chemicals," and the absolute handling of biological or chemical hazards remain fully active. Sabotaging an asteroid run, contaminating spaceport shipping lanes, or abandoning volatile nuclear/chemical thruster elements due to gross greed or personal ineptitude is legally classified as high treason.

Article 3002: Statutory Economy of Commercial Rules
The state will not clutter the code with excessive, bureaucratic industry-specific red tape or overlapping commercial zoning regulations.

*The Principle of Regulatory Minimalism: Outside of life-critical resource preservation, environmental protections, and the unyielding defense of worker rights, the state enforces no separate, specialized business codes on raw material extraction or asset salvage.

*The Open Frontier Baseline: Provided an enterprise operates with absolute transparency (Title XVII), strictly adheres to the flat 25% corporate tax assessment (Title XXIX), and honors the absolute prohibition against corporate personhood and lobbying (Title VI), they are entirely free to mine, salvage, and trade resources without arbitrary state micro-management. If an activity does not hurt an individual, exploit a worker, or threaten the infrastructure of the colony, the state shall not interfere.

Article 3002: Transponder Mandates and Emergency Safety Requirements
To safeguard life across the hazardous vacuum of space and ensure clear traffic monitoring, all vessels operating under Martian jurisdiction must maintain strict hardware baseline redundancies:

*The Continuous Transponder Requirement: Every Martian-registered vessel, whether planetbound atmosphere craft, surface rovers, orbital shuttles, or deep-space asteroid mining rigs or otherwise, must be equipped with a functional, active navigation transponder. Deactivating a transponder within the Martian zone of influence without explicit state authorization is illegal.

*The SOS Emergency Beacon Mandate: All vessels must possess an isolated, independent emergency SOS distress beacon capable of broadcasting automated coordinate location data even in the event of a catastrophic primary power failure.

*The Emergency Life Pod Provision: Every vessel must maintain a sufficient quantity of certified, fully stocked emergency life pods or structural escape modules to accommodate 100% of the active crew and passengers on board. These pods must feature independent life-support networks capable of sustaining life until localized rescue operations can be executed.

Article 3003: The Spaceborne Good Samaritan Mandate
To anchor a high-trust society across the empty expanse of the vacuum, the state establishes an absolute humanitarian baseline for all vessels traversing the Martian zone of influence:

*The Mandatory Attempted Rescue Rule: Any vessel operating within the planet's vicinity or immediate sector that intercepts an emergency broadcast, distress beacon, or active hazard transmission is legally required to attempt a rescue. The master or crew must deploy a proactive, good-faith effort to save stranded vessels, malfunctioning life pods, or drifting individuals.

*The Sovereign Safety Threshold: The legal obligation to render aid is strictly bound by operational viability. A vessel is explicitly exempt from executing a rescue maneuver if the physics or circumstances of the crisis would directly place the rescuing ship, its primary structural hull, or its own onboard crew and passengers into immediate, un-manageable danger

*The No-Fee Baseline: All emergency life-saving assistance, physical extractions, medical stabilizations, and temporary habitat boarding operations executed under this mandate must be provided completely free of charge. Commercializing a vacuum casualty or demanding immediate financial salvage parameters before saving a sapient life is unconstitutional, and is considered piracy.

*The Fuel Compensation Override: While the service itself is free, the state recognizes that executing burns, orbital adjustments, or multi-ton acceleration corrections drains vital ship resources. If the state determines the rescue operation was legitimate, necessary, and carried out in good faith, the public port authority may fully compensate the rescuing entity with state-backed fuel allowances, propellant re-fills, or Martian Credits to unburden their supply ledger.


Article 3004: Orbital Defense Debris, Salvage Rights, and Emergency Containment
To ensure clear property boundaries and maintain orbital path safety following defensive actions, the state establishes strict protocols for ship remains within the Martian zone of influence:

*The State Ownership Rule: Any vessel that is destroyed, neutralized, or blown up by planetary orbital defense systems for any reason is immediately classified as absolute state property. This ownership extends to all structural hulls, advanced technology, refined metals, automated subsystems, and cargo contents remaining in the debris field.

*Discretionary Salvage Contracts: The state reserves the sole right to issue debris collection, sweeping, or mineral mining licenses to private citizens or legal entities. The scope, duration, and financial parameters of these salvage agreements are decided entirely on a case-by-case basis by port administrators.

*The Unauthorized Access Prohibition: Venturing into a state-owned debris field, harvesting wreckage, or tampering with destroyed vessels without an explicit, active state license is strictly illegal and prosecuted under the colony's anti-fraud and anti-theft statutes.

*The Dynamic Collision Exception: A vessel is completely exempt from the unauthorized access penalty if active fragments or debris fields float directly into the flight trajectory of an incoming ship that is physically or mechanically unable to alter its course.

*The Immediate Aftermath Containment Rule: If an enforcement action occurs near other law-abiding, legal vessels, those ships are legally permitted to actively intercept, lasso, or contain the flying debris to prevent structural collisions or hull damage. This protective containment is completely legal provided that absolutely no technology, cargo, or material is stolen, hidden, or pocketed by the rescuing craft. All contained debris must be turned over to state marshals upon the arrival of authority vessels.

Title XXXI: Military Operations, Jurisdictions, and Tactical Exceptions
=======================================================

Article 3101: Operational Exemptions and Domestic ConstraintsTo ensure planetary readiness and defense capability, the state military and sovereign aerospace forces operate under specialized tactical parameters:

*Exclusionary Zone Bypass: All active military personnel and sovereign vessels are entirely exempt from localized no-go zones, state-owned debris fields (Article 3004), or restricted flight paths when executing active tactical orders or clandestine missions. This exemption is strictly unconstitutional if the operation attempts to pierce, monitor, or violate the civil rights of Martian citizens (Article 2708).

*The Anti-Loitering Clause: The military is strictly banned from occupying civilian airspace or lingering inside restricted zones without a definitive, documented, and verifiable operational reason. The armed forces shall never be used as a permanent occupying shadow over civilian life.


*The Transponder Exception: Sovereign military vessels are not bound by standard civilian space transponder regulations (Article 3002). Fleet command retains absolute authority to deactivate tracking metrics or deploy encrypted stealth signatures to safeguard covert positioning or planetary defense readiness.

Article 3102: Armed Mandate and Vessel Inspection Protocols
The military serves as the primary enforcement arm of state sovereignty and planetary security:

*The Projectile Weapon Allowance: Active military personnel are explicitly authorized to possess, transport, and open-carry kinetic projectile firearms and explosive weapon systems while on duty and executing their professional assignments. This exception directly mirrors the environmental protections of Article 301.

*The Discretionary Boarding Mandate: Military forces maintain the absolute right to intercept, board, and thoroughly inspect any incoming or transiting vessel for any reason—or for no publicly stated reason.

*The Standard of Reasonableness: All military boarding actions must remain entirely reasonable and must never be leveraged to subvert or violate the constitutional rights, private property, or data privacy of law-abiding citizens.

Article 3103: High-Risk Rescue Duties and Hazardous Research Boundaries
As a state-backed apparatus, the military bears an elevated burden to preserve sapient life and advance tactical science:

*The High-Risk Emergency Standard: Unlike civilian or commercial craft bound by the safety thresholds of Article 3003, military vessels must attempt to render aid and execute rescue maneuvers during active SOS emergencies even when the situation carries significant operational risk.

*The Terminal Limit: A military commander may only withhold rescue efforts if executing the maneuver would definitively and inevitably compromise the physical survival of the crew and the core structural integrity of the vessel itself.

*Hazardous Research Entitlement: The military apparatus remains strictly subject to the life-critical environmental protections, planetary safety constraints, and "forever chemical" bans outlined in Title XVIII and Title XIX. However, the state shall not arbitrarily block, prevent, or stall the military from conducting authorized, high-security scientific research on hazardous elements or defensive materials without a clear, definitive, and urgent public safety reason.

Article 3104: Discharge Protocol, Clearance Stripping, and State Secret Accountability
To prevent the leakage of classified technology, operational parameters, or planetary defense vulnerabilities, the separation of military personnel from active service shall follow an absolute operational boundary:

*The Total Stripping of Clearance: Any individual leaving the military—regardless of the reason whatsoever, including honorable discharge, retirement, medical separation, or resignation—shall have all security clearances, tactical accesses, and cryptographic permissions immediately and permanently stripped.

*The Civilian Reclassification Baseline: Upon discharge, the former serviceman is legally reclassified as an ordinary civilian regarding all state secrets. They hold zero ongoing right to access, discuss, or receive updates on classified military data.

*The Continuous Treason Liability: Returning to civilian life does not dissolve prior military non-disclosure obligations. If a discharged individual deliberately shares, sells, or leaks state secrets or classified military data acquired during their service, they violate their Article 003 oath and commit a Tier 3 High Crime against the state, carrying the mandatory capital execution penalty.

Article 3105: Defense Procurement Standards and Corporate Fraud Penalties
To protect public funds and maintain absolute defense readiness, all private military contractors, defense enterprises, and weapon manufacturers are bound to strict operational performance metrics:

*The Direct Contract Mandate: Defense contractors must deliver all systems, vessels, and services exactly as specified in the signed state agreement. The state will not tolerate corporate bloat, lazy execution, incompetence, or inefficient habits that lead to non-deliveries or hardware performing below baseline metrics. State contracts are not mechanisms for corporations to grow fat and lazy; working for the state military is a distinct privilege and a matter of high prestige.

*The Engineering Extension Exception: Timeline extensions or budget adjustments are strictly illegal unless an independent state audit proves that entirely unforeseen engineering requirements or raw physical limitations necessitated the delay.

*The Fraud Classification: Any defense contractor found intentionally cutting corners, inflating costs, delivering substandard gear, or failing to meet deadlines due to organizational incompetence shall be prosecuted for criminal fraud. In alignment with Article 006, this is classified as a Tier 2 Intermediate Offense at minimum, or a Tier 3 High Crime if it directly compromises planetary defense.

*The Universal Asset Clawback: Upon a finding of defense procurement fraud, the state shall aggressively claw back the entire financial worth of the contract. The state shall also immediately confiscate all produced physical materials, manufacturing tools, digital schematics, prototypes, and intellectual property developed during the contract, transferring them entirely to state ownership.

*The Total Silencing Provision: Under the strict penalty of high treason (Tier 3), any contractor, executive, or technician involved in a fraudulent or terminated defense contract is permanently barred from discussing, publishing, or sharing any aspect of the work performed. Any leak of this structural data carries the mandatory execution penalty.

Article 3106: Absolute Classification, Budget Aggregation, and Supreme Oversight
To maintain a high-trust society while safeguarding critical tactical advantages, the state balances public financial transparency with absolute operational security:

*The Supreme Right of Classification: The state retains the absolute, non-negotiable right to classify any military contract, hardware project, personnel roster, weapon system, intellectual property, or operational asset under military jurisdiction. This classification can be enacted for any reason or for no publicly stated reason, placing the asset on a strict, compartmented need-to-know basis.

*The Single Line-Item Mandate: The general public possesses an unalienable right to monitor state finances and track exactly how much total revenue is allocated to defensive operations. To preserve this right without leaking classified intelligence, all black-budget or classified expenditures must be aggregated into a single, comprehensive financial total. This total shall be displayed on the public state ledger as a single, opaque line item containing the aggregate cost and nothing more.

*The Public Exclusion Standard: While the public has every right to know the financial weight of these hidden operations, they possess zero right whatsoever to look into the specifics, schematics, locations, or personnel lists of classified projects. This restriction is absolute unless an individual is explicitly empowered via specific, top-tier military clearance—an occurrence made highly unlikely due to the strict structural compartmentalization of the defense network.

*Absolute Supreme Oversight: Because the highest state leader is chosen directly by the populace, democratic oversight must remain supreme over the military apparatus. The democratically elected supreme official of the state holds the absolute constitutional right to know anything, everything, and all hidden metrics within the military budget. No general, intelligence director, or defense contractor may hide any detail, black project, or classified expense from the supreme leader. Doing so is treason against the public.
